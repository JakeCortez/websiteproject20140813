About Us

We are a non-profit charity founded to assist children and young adults suffering from Polio. We use donated money to purchase and deliver vaccines to underdevoloped countries, fund research for cures, and aquiring equipment to assist people suffering from acute flaccid paralysis, a rare but severe symptom of polio.
